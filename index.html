About ORDO Tours

At ORDO Tours, we don't just organize trips — we guide you into the heart of the Silk Road. Our tours are crafted for curious travelers who seek authenticity, raw beauty, and cultural connection. Sleep in yurts under the stars, ride through alpine meadows, and sip tea with real nomads.
